Paint
Correction

Paint correction is the art of removing paint defects through a controlled, multi-stage machine polishing process. Our certified specialists use DA and rotary polishers with precision-matched compounds and polishes to eliminate swirl marks, fine scratches, water etching, and oxidation — recovering true paint clarity.

1-stage, 2-stage & 3-stage correction available
Swirl mark and scratch removal
Water spot and etching removal
Oxidation restoration
Paint depth measurement included
Pre and post gloss meter readings
Starting from $499
Book Paint Correction

Film

Self-healing TPU technology
Full-body or partial zone coverage
Rock chip and road debris protection
Computer-cut precision templates
