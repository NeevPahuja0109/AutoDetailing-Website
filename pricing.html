Frequently Asked Questions

How long does each service take?
+
Essential Detail: 2–3 hours. Premium Detail: 4–6 hours. Ceramic Package: 2–3 days (paint correction + cure time). Ultimate Protection: 5–7 days. We'll always give you a precise timeline when you book.
Do you offer mobile detailing?
+
We currently operate exclusively from our Miami studio facility. This allows us to maintain controlled conditions — optimal temperature, humidity, and lighting — essential for ceramic coatings, paint correction, and PPF installation.
What brands of ceramic coating do you use?
+
We exclusively use professional-grade coatings including Gtechniq Crystal Serum Ultra, IGL Kenzo, and Gyeon Quartz Mohs EVO. These are installer-exclusive products not available to the public, providing significantly superior performance and durability.
How do I maintain a ceramic-coated vehicle?
+
Maintenance is straightforward — hand wash every 2–4 weeks using a pH-neutral shampoo, avoid automatic car washes, and bring it back for our complimentary annual inspection. We'll handle any top-coat maintenance needed to keep your coating performing at its best.
Do you work on all vehicle makes?
+
Yes. We work on all vehicles — from daily drivers to Lamborghinis and Bugattis. We have extensive experience with exotics, EVs, matte finishes, and wrap-applied vehicles. Each vehicle receives a thorough inspection before any work begins.
Is paint correction necessary before ceramic coating?
+
Yes — ceramic coating permanently locks in whatever condition the paint is in. Any existing swirl marks, scratches, or water spots will be sealed under the coating. We include the appropriate level of paint correction in all our ceramic packages to ensure a perfect foundation.
